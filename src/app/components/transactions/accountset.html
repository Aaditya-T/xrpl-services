Account Flags:

Require Destination Tag You can change only one Account Flag at a time. Attention! Transactions without Destination Tag will be rejected! Attention! Users will be able to send transactions without Destination Tag!
Disable Master Key You can change only one Account Flag at a time. Before you can disable your Master Key you have to set an alternative signing method. (Regular Key or SignerList) Attention! You are going to disable the Master Key of your account. You could loose access to your account. Make sure you know what you are doing! Attention! You are going to reactivate your Master Key! If it was compromised, attackers have the possibility to steal your funds!
Default Ripple You can change only one Account Flag at a time. Careful! You are going to set the Default Ripple flag to your account. This enables rippling on new Trust Lines by default! You are going to disable the Default Ripple flag of your account. New Trust Lines won't allow rippling by default anymore!
You can change only one Account Flag at a time. To change two account flags, you have to execute two transactions.
